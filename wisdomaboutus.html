HOME  ABOUT US

Wisdom Skills Academy is a vocational and technical training centre for the advancement of knowledge and medium scale enterprises

It is a non for profit making initiative; birthed to raise the manpower base of our nation, reduce unemployment, poverty and thuggery and other social vices associated and inimical with unemployment

Our mandate is to reduce the menace of unemployment by training youths in various skills; thereby ensuring that they are self reliant, trainers in skills and providers of jobs

Our Core Values

  • Creativity
  • Productivit
  • Solution-driven
  • Reliability

Our Vision

Our vision is that in a few years, we become the skills acquisition centre of choice, delivering quality training, raising entrepreneurs, and improving wealth creation opportunities for young people all over the continent of Africa.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower young people with entrepreneurial and leadership skills to give them a competitive edge in a constantly evolving world.
